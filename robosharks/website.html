About Our Website

Our website was created by student team members for the FIRST robotics competition. The team members that worked on the website first talked to a company by the name of Realmac Software. The company was gracious enough to donate there amazing web editing software for our team to use.

So how does it work exactly? Well, this software helps keep the content of our web pages and the website design separate. Rapidweaver, the software that was donated, can achieve this separation of content and design, by allowing its users to create what are called themes. These themes are very powerful tools that can set up a webpage inside and out, virtually everything except for the main content of the page. While looking into website solutions the Robo-Sharks website designers came across an amazing template that used what is called Ajax. This theme was created by a web developer by the name of Robbie-White, and he called his theme Jaxs Pro. He also was very gracious and donated his theme to us to use how we would like. So now in our hands was an amazing software and a beautiful theme. But we wanted to take it one step further and completely redesign our website from the ground up. To be able to edit the endless amount of stylesheets and html documents we would need an amazingly powerful text editor. That is when we asked MacroMates if they would like to donate their awesome text editor so that we could recreate our website.

Having all this software we then began creating a Rapidweaver theme from scratch, naming it “JaxsPro1612”. We then took the Ajax page loading feature that was donated in the original Jaxs Pro theme, if you notice we kept the cool Jaxs Pro name. Then we completely designed a webpage that best represented our team in every light. The new design uses the entire viewing window to display all of the amazing content that we have to offer. Also we upgraded our picture viewing experience to a brand new look and feel that is sure to just pop out at you.

One last thing that we would like to point out to all of those in the FIRST community is that we have also completed Deans homework assignment for the 2009-2010 season. As a quick reminder his homework was to create a database or a reference of all alumni for your team. So we did just that, we found every last person that was ever part of our robotics team. You can click here to see a page of all of our alumni.

So while you browse through our website we hope that you enjoy all that it is we have to offer you, and also to send us feedback by clicking the Give Feedback link at the bottom of all of our pages.

If you would like to give us a pat on the back and tell us nice work you may contact us using the subject line “Two Thumbs Up!” directly at RoboSharks1612@gmail.com or by visiting our contact page.
